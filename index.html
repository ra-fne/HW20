A work preview - my very first UX research project with Tara

Post-Pandemic Travel

We have been commissioned by a new travel Start-up, JetOFF, to design a new mobile app that helps busy people to plan their trips for the post-pandemic era. Not just equipped with booking functions, the users can also opt in to receive local COVID and attractions/ events updates for the selected destination(s), it also allows them to add these information onto their own personal calendar by just 1 click, an instantly updated itinerary and a to-do list to get them all ready to go.

We decided to create this app to target young corporate personnel who likes to travel and wants to be on top of all parts of life as much as possible while enjoyinig a well-deserved holiday.

User Research - Proto Persona

33, a single professional

curious, humble and patient

friendly and open-minded

loves spending time with family and friends

Behavioural Demographic

love to travel and meet new people

appreciate learning about different cultures

up-to-date with news internationally and nationally

tech whiz

financially stable, willing to save and spend

Goals and Needs

+ travel to visit family and friends

+ explore the different culture

+ stay within budget while not sacrificing styles

+ return to work as scheduled

Pain points/ Potential Solutions

- often miss out ad hoc deals by not spotting them fast enough on social media

- dates to travel are usually fixed each year but may have sudden change due to unexpected work arise

- bookings amendments are difficult or not available

- no direct contact with service provider when needed

- no obvious option on return or rebooking

- concern about service quality and value of money

Interview Insights

“I think I'm feeling quite excited about it (travelling internationally) in terms of I'll get to visit my family again, however I do worry about the chance of getting infected by COVID during the flight or even during the quarantine period because of those other environments that I cannot control.” ~ Joyce, 28

“I think it is very helpful (to use technology) these days ‘coz we can book everything online, accommodations, transport, restaurant, insurance, flight ticket, everything, so I think it is really really helpful and you don’t actually have to call somebody and wait on (the) line and you can just sort your things out at your own time, I think it is really helpful.” ~ Abbie, 33

“Hesitancy..covid outbreak fears..vaccines good new norm and start herd immunity. Postandemic travel should include quarantining at home.” ~ anonymous, 55

By listening to our interviewees, we have a deeper understanding about our users.We understand more about their goals and concerns. We know what their attractions and dissatisfactions about using technology. We also learnt about the functions and process they want to use. All of this will be considered when creating the future, indispensable product for JetOFF

Affinity Diagram

We collected all the insights from our interviews to create this Affinity Diagram. Easing worries, being able to handle sudden changes and staying on top of the latest news are the loudest opinions we can hear.

Empathy Map

GAIN

Having the opportunity to travel overseas/interstate again

I know all the coronavirus safety updates and safety procedures when entering a country

THINK

How can I make the most within my budget?
